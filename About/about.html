My Story

During my early childhood living in a quiet isolated rural area outside of a small town in Illinois surrounded by corn fields, meadows and highways. Most days, I would ride my bike to the edge of my street and gaze at the many trucks and cars that would wiz by and imagining the cities and adventures that awaits them. Meanwhile, my imagination never ceased even in the quiet confines of my room where I would play with my dolls giving them complicated characters to enact in my cardboard-made dollhouse. My dreams of acting and creating never stopped. It is what my soul craves and needs to survive. My name is JassyBlu. I am a creative artist; a thespian with a flair for designing using imagery from the deepest depths of my imagination to bring inspiration and wonder to those who are fortunate to view my work.
