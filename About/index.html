My Story

During my early childhood living in a rural area outside of a small town in Illinois surrounded by corn fields, meadows and an Interstate highway that would have a rapid succession of trucks and cars. I would ride my bike to the edge of it, gaze at them as they whizzed by, imagining which big cities they would be heading to. Meanwhile, my imagination never ceased even in the quiet confines of my room where I would play with my dolls and create stories which they would bring the characters to life. My dreams of acting and creating never stopped. It is what my soul craves and needs to survive. My name is JassyBlu. I am a creative artist; a thespian with a flair for designing using imagery from the deepest depths of my imagination to bring inspiration and wonder to those who are fortunate to view my work on canvas or on stage.
